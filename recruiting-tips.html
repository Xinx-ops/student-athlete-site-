Recruiting Tips for High School Student-Athletes

How to get noticed by college coaches and take your athletic career to the next level.

Back to Home

1. Build a Highlight Reel

Record your best games or drills. Keep it short (2–3 minutes) and highlight your strengths. Include your name, position, school, and contact info at the start.

2. Research Colleges and Coaches

Look for schools that match your academic and athletic goals. Make a list of coaches and programs, then reach out professionally via email or social media.

3. Maintain Strong Academics

Coaches look for athletes who can succeed in the classroom. Keep your GPA strong and track standardized test scores.

4. Attend Showcases and Camps

Participate in tournaments, showcases, and camps to get exposure. Bring your highlight reel and contact info to share with coaches.

5. Be Professional Online

Many coaches check social media. Keep profiles clean and share content that shows your commitment and discipline.
